You must implement a structured hiring capacity system based on office size and branch expansion.

HARD RULES:
- DO NOT change UI layout
- DO NOT break hiring system
- DO NOT remove existing team roles
- DO NOT affect salary or bonus systems
- ONLY control hiring limits and capacity rules

GOAL:
Create a realistic hiring progression system tied to company growth.

----------------------------------------
PART 1 — BASE LIMIT
----------------------------------------

Set initial hiring limit:

- Max employees = 10

This applies when:
- no branches exist
- no office upgrades applied

----------------------------------------
PART 2 — BRANCH EXPANSION SYSTEM
----------------------------------------

Each "Sales Office Branch" adds:

+3 employees to TOTAL company capacity

Examples:

0 branches → 10 employees
1 branch → 13 employees
2 branches → 16 employees
3 branches → 19 employees

----------------------------------------
PART 3 — OFFICE UPGRADE SYSTEM
----------------------------------------

Each office upgrade adds:

+1 employee PER SECTOR

Example sectors:
- Development
- Marketing
- Legal
- Others if they exist

This increases internal capacity but must respect total cap.

----------------------------------------
PART 4 — HIRING VALIDATION
----------------------------------------

Before hiring:

1. Check total employees
2. Compare with total capacity
3. If limit reached:
   - block hiring
   - show message:
     "Expand your company to hire more employees"

----------------------------------------
PART 5 — UI FEEDBACK
----------------------------------------

Display current capacity:

Example:
"Employees: 10 / 13"

Update dynamically when:
- hiring
- firing
- opening branch
- upgrading office

----------------------------------------
SAFETY
----------------------------------------

- Do not affect:
  - bonuses
  - salaries
  - production systems
  - marketing systems

----------------------------------------
AFTER
----------------------------------------

- confirm base limit is active
- confirm branches increase capacity
- confirm upgrades increase per-sector capacity
- confirm hiring is properly blocked when limit reached
