   "Balanço Financeiro"

   - Caixa atual
   - Receita mensal
   - Despesas mensais

   - Despesas acumuladas

     year
     month
     revenue
     expenses
     profit
     cash

   - income and losses should feel more realistic
   - revenue must come from actual sources like:
     game sales
     console sales
     sponsorship/contracts if present
     other market income if already implemented
   - expenses must include:
     employee costs
     development costs
     marketing costs
     production/support costs
     other active systems if present

   netIncome = totalRevenue - totalExpenses

   - low-quality or poorly managed products should hurt finances more

   - growth should feel earned, not automatic

   - use compact cards
   - clean labels
   - bars or small indicators where helpful
   - dark theme consistent with the rest of the app

8. Optional but recommended:
   - add a small chart or progress-style visual for monthly profit trend
   - add color states:
     green = healthy
     yellow = warning
     red = loss/crisis

9. Keep save/load compatibility:
   - persist finance history safely
   - do not break existing saves

Only update what is necessary for:
   - financial analysis UI

   - monthly financial calculation
   - balance of money gain/loss

11. Explain clearly after changes:
   - which files were changed
