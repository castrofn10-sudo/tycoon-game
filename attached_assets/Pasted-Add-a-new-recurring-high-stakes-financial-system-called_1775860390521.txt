Add a new recurring high-stakes financial system called:

"Investidor Sombrio" (Shadow Investor)

GOAL:
Create a dramatic bailout mechanic that can save the player during financial collapse, but always creates long-term consequences and future tension.

IMPORTANT:
- Keep this system rare
- Do not make it feel unfair
- It must only appear in serious crises
- Make it memorable and strategic
- Keep UI mobile-friendly

====================================
1. TRIGGER CONDITIONS
====================================

The Shadow Investor event should only be eligible when ALL or MOST of these are true:

- Company balance is negative (red)
- Player has no active loan available OR loan limit reached
- Player has no major active product/project likely to generate enough income soon
- Monthly cash flow is very weak or negative
- Bankruptcy risk is high

This should feel like a true "last chance" rescue.

====================================
2. FIRST EVENT – SHADOW OFFER
====================================

Create a dramatic event:

Title:
"Proposta Anônima"

Tone:
Mysterious, tense, corporate, dangerous

Example flavor:
- "Uma entidade desconhecida entrou em contato."
- "Nenhum nome. Nenhum rosto. Só números."
- "Eles sabem que sua empresa está à beira do colapso."
- "A ajuda chegou... mas não gratuitamente."

Player options:
A) Aceitar ajuda
B) Recusar proposta

====================================
3. IF PLAYER ACCEPTS
====================================

Immediate bailout effect:
- Clear debt OR inject a strong emergency bailout
- Prevent immediate bankruptcy
- Stabilize the company temporarily

Then secretly or explicitly create a shadow contract.

Randomly assign one of these consequences:

------------------------------------
A) TAKE SHARES / EQUITY DEAL
------------------------------------
- Investor takes 10% to 30% of the company
- Apply long-term effect:
  - reduce future profits by a percentage
  - reduce net earnings slightly every cycle
  - optionally reduce company control in future systems

------------------------------------
B) DEBT COLLECTION + 30% INTEREST
------------------------------------
- Investor records the bailout as hidden debt
- When the company becomes financially healthy again:
  - automatically trigger collection event
  - charge original bailout + 30%

------------------------------------
C) PERFORMANCE DEMAND CONTRACT
------------------------------------
- Investor demands aggressive performance
- For a period of time:
  - if company underperforms, extra penalties apply
  - if company performs well, penalty is lighter

====================================
4. IF PLAYER REFUSES
====================================

- No bailout
- Company remains in danger
- Bankruptcy risk increases
- Add dramatic feedback text

====================================
5. FOLLOW-UP EVENT SYSTEM
====================================

The Shadow Investor must not disappear forever.

Later, when the company improves financially, trigger a return event.

Titles:
- "Eles Voltaram"
- "A Conta Chegou"
- "Cobrança Silenciosa"
- "Contrato Executado"

Depending on the original deal:
- take profits / shares
- collect debt + 30%
- impose performance penalty
- demand payout at inconvenient timing

====================================
6. RECURRING NARRATIVE PRESENCE
====================================

The Shadow Investor can return more than once across a long save.

Rules:
- very low frequency
- only during major financial arcs
- should feel like a looming corporate force

Possible narrative identity:
- unknown investment fund
- hidden rival holding group
- silent financial predator
- faceless corporate syndicate

Keep identity mysterious.

====================================
7. UI / PRESENTATION
====================================

Make these events visually distinct:
- darker warning style
- premium dramatic card
- stronger event framing
- memorable wording

It should feel different from normal incidents.

====================================
8. BALANCE RULES
====================================

- Do NOT let this become exploitable
- Do NOT let player use it as free money
- Consequences must matter
- It should be a desperate survival choice

Recommended:
- cooldown before another appearance
- stronger consequence if used repeatedly

====================================
9. FLAVOR TEXT / IMMERSION
====================================

Add atmospheric flavor lines like:
- "Nenhum nome. Nenhum rosto. Só uma proposta."
- "Eles não investem em empresas. Eles compram destinos."
- "O dinheiro entrou em segundos. O preço virá depois."
- "Você foi salvo. Talvez esse tenha sido o erro."
- "Algumas dívidas não ficam no balanço. Ficam na sombra."

====================================
10. GAMEPLAY IMPACT
====================================

This system should add:
- tension
- strategy
- narrative depth
- fear of easy rescue
- memorable corporate storytelling

====================================
11. FINAL CHECK
====================================

After implementation:
- ensure event only appears in true crisis
- ensure consequences trigger correctly later
- ensure no crashes
- ensure the mechanic feels rare, dramatic, and interesting
