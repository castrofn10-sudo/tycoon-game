You must enhance the Acquisitions system by adding resale mechanics and a dynamic sponsorship system, without breaking balance or existing systems.

HARD RULES:
- DO NOT change core gameplay systems
- DO NOT create guaranteed profit mechanics
- DO NOT break economic balance
- DO NOT remove existing acquisition features
- ONLY extend behavior with realistic logic
- Keep all systems stable and scalable

GOAL:
Allow players to sell acquisitions dynamically and introduce a flexible sponsorship system with realistic risk/reward.

IMPLEMENTATION:

1. Resale system for acquisitions:
- All acquisitions (except Charity) can be sold
- Selling price must be dynamic and based on:
  - performance over time
  - market conditions
  - internal success/failure logic

2. Resale behavior:
- Possible outcomes:
  - profit (sell above purchase price)
  - loss (sell below purchase price)
- Variation must feel realistic and not random-only
- Each category behaves differently:
  - Sports teams: based on performance
  - Mobile companies: growth or decline cycles
  - Cinema/media: tied to success of releases
  - Technology: more stable valuation
  - Charity: cannot be sold

3. Sponsorship system:
- Generate dynamic sponsorship opportunities across the game
- Types of events may include:
  - tournaments
  - tech expos
  - gaming events
  - cultural festivals
  - global launches
  - award shows

4. Sponsorship investment:
- Player chooses how much to invest (up to 50M)

5. Sponsorship outcomes:
- Profit range: +1% to +12%
- Loss range: -1% to -30%
- Results depend on:
  - event scale
  - timing
  - market conditions
  - light randomness

6. Event scaling:
- Larger events:
  - higher potential profit
  - higher risk
- Smaller events:
  - lower profit
  - more stable outcomes

7. UI behavior:
- Add "Sell" option to acquisition cards
- Show estimated value range before selling
- Sponsorship events appear as opportunities (similar to events/news)

8. Balance:
- No guaranteed profit systems
- Maintain risk vs reward in all categories
- Ensure economy remains stable over long gameplay

AFTER:
- confirm resale system is active
- confirm values change over time
- confirm sponsorship system generates dynamic events
- confirm no system became overpowered
- confirm economy balance is preserved
