Improve the in-game news system to be smarter, less repetitive, less frequent, and more meaningful.

Requirements:

1. Reduce overall news frequency.
   - News should feel important, not constant spam.
   - Avoid generating too many news items every month.

2. Make rival/company attack events much less frequent.
   - Rival attacks should happen randomly, with larger cooldowns.
   - Use a randomized cooldown between 4 and 8 months on average.
   - They must not happen too often or in back-to-back months.
   - Add a global cooldown so attack-type news feels rare and impactful.

3. Improve news variety and intelligence.
   Add more meaningful market/world news related to:
   - stock market movement
   - company growth
   - company decline
   - bankruptcies
   - acquisitions
   - console launches
   - major game launches
   - market booms and slowdowns
   - hype waves and consumer trends
   - reputation shifts
   - research breakthroughs
   - investor pressure
   - fan demand changes

4. News should depend on actual game state.
   Generate news based on:
   - company reputation
   - fans
   - market share
   - released products
   - active consoles
   - competitor health
   - bankruptcies
   - recent launches
   - stock/investor systems
   - market phase/year

5. Make the system less repetitive.
   - Avoid reusing the same message patterns too often.
   - Use more variation in titles and bodies.
   - Add multiple templates per category.
   - Prevent the same category from appearing repeatedly in short intervals.

6. Improve news quality over quantity.
   - Fewer news items, but each one should feel more interesting and useful.
   - Important events should be highlighted more clearly.
   - Minor events should be rarer or grouped.

7. Keep performance good.
   - Do not create unnecessary news objects every tick.
   - Only generate news when conditions are truly met.
   - Avoid heavy loops or excessive random checks.

8. Keep the current save/load and UI system compatible.
   - Do not break persistence.
   - Do not break existing NewsItem structure unless safely extended.

9. Add a clean balancing system for news categories:
   - attack news = rare
   - market news = occasional
   - launch news = event-driven
   - bankruptcy news = event-driven
   - stock/company movement news = occasional and state-based

   - which files were changed
   - how frequency was reduced
   - how cooldowns work
   - how variety was improved
